Pat's Salmon Cookies

Thank you for visiting our website! We had a dream to make the best salmon cookies in the Puget Sound area. We worked long and hard to come up with a recipe that we thought would trustly capture the flavor of Seattle. Unfortunately, people really do not like salmon in their cookies! We got frustrated and tossed the cookies in the garbage. The next morning, to our surprise, we had about 2 dozen dogs nosing through our garbage! They love the salmon cookies! So, we decided to change our business model and now we make salmon cookies for dogs!
